The Signal
Il Segnale

L'emozione è rumore. Questo è il segnale.

Un processo di monitoraggio analizza ogni ora le notizie che muovono realmente l'oro — Fed, FOMC, CPI/PCE/NFP, macro globale, geopolitica. Un filtro rigido elimina speculazione e notizie stantie. Ciò che resta diventa un numero preciso che guida ogni decisione di posizionamento.

— Reading the signal…
Direction — 0 = bearish · 50 = neutral · 100 = bullish
Strength — how strong the signal is, regardless of direction

Updates automatically — no manual input.

  • Pesato su ciò che muove davvero l'oro

    Ogni notizia viene classificata per categoria e importanza: Fed policy → macro chiave → geopolitica → macro secondaria → generale — con bias rialzista, ribassista o neutro.

  • Time-decay, non soglie rigide

    L'influenza di una notizia si dimezza ogni sei ore circa: il segnale scivola con fluidità invece di saltare quando un elemento invecchia ed esce dal calcolo.

  • Dal segnale alla posizione

    Il sistema legge il segnale per ogni ordine pendente e regola il livello di rischio — basso, medio o alto — e la distanza del take-profit. I trade contro il segnale corrente usano un tier fisso separato e più conservativo.

Fed policy → Key macro → Geopolitics → Time decay → Position sizing

The System
Il Sistema

Nessuna esitazione. Nessuna vendetta. Nessun ripensamento.

AuremAI analizza il mercato in profondità, posiziona ordini pending ai livelli chiave di liquidità ed entra solo quando il prezzo conferma il movimento. Le posizioni aperte sono gestite con uno stop trailing dinamico — protegge il profitto e lascia correre i vincitori. Zero interferenza emotiva.

  • TP & SL on every trade
  • No martingale
  • No grid system
Execution loop
  1. 1 Map liquidity zones
  2. 2 Place pending order
  3. 3 Wait for confirmation
  4. 4 Trail stop & manage exit
Entry style Breakout confirmed fills only — no market chasing
Risk model Fixed / % lot size or % equity per trade
  • 01

    Esecuzione chirurgica

    Gli ordini pending vengono posizionati a livelli strutturali precisi. Il sistema aspetta la conferma del prezzo prima di entrare — nessun inseguimento, nessun ingresso impulsivo.

  • 02

    Protezione dinamica

    Una volta in trade, uno stop trailing dinamico blocca i guadagni mentre il movimento si sviluppa — senza tagliare i vincitori troppo presto.

  • 03

    Rischio su misura

    Personalizzabile: lot size fisso o rischio percentuale per sfruttare la crescita composta nel tempo — alle tue condizioni.

Liquidity zones → Pending order → Confirmation → Trailing stop → Take profit / SL

Un approccio strutturato, controllato e professionale — costruito per operare in modo strategico, senza stress, senza emozioni.

FAQ

Questions, answered straight.

Everything you need to evaluate AuremAI before you connect an account — setup, risk, delivery, and how the macro signal drives position sizing.

01

What is AuremAI?

AuremAI is AI-enhanced trading software for gold (XAUUSD) — a breakout and liquidity zone strategy that reads a live macro signal to size every position. What you're buying is access to run the system on your MT5 terminal, delivered and supported over Telegram.

02

Does it use martingale or a grid?

No. AuremAI does not use martingale or grid logic. Every trade is opened with predefined take profit and stop loss, and risk is managed through fixed lot size or percentage-based sizing — your choice.

03

How is the signal calculated?

A monitoring process scans for high-relevance gold news every few hours and tags each item with a category (Fed policy carries the most weight, down through key macro, major geopolitics, secondary macro, and general) and a bullish, bearish, or neutral bias. A live endpoint blends recent items using time-decay weighting — influence halves roughly every six hours — into a single bias label, a directional score, and a strength reading.

04

What instrument does it trade?

Gold only — XAUUSD on MetaTrader 5. The strategy, macro news filter, and execution logic are built and tuned for that single market rather than spread across unrelated pairs.

05

Can I see results before I buy?

Yes. The Track Record section above shows a live trading account — equity, balance, and history — that updates on its own. No purchase or sign-up required to watch it.

06

What risk is involved?

Trading carries real risk, including the loss of capital. AuremAI sizes positions to manage risk — using low, medium, and high risk tiers depending on signal strength — but managing risk is not the same as removing it. No system can guarantee a profit, and you should never trade money you can't afford to lose.

07

No. AuremAI provides software and market signals, not personalised investment advice. You remain responsible for your account, broker relationship, and trading decisions. Consult a licensed adviser if you need regulated guidance.

The system can run on any MT5 broker, but our live track record is verified on VT Markets. To get the closest match to the performance shown on this page, we strongly recommend registering through the partner link we send you on Telegram — same execution environment as our monitored account.

There is no fixed minimum published here — it depends on your broker's margin rules and how you size risk. We recommend enough balance to absorb normal drawdown without breaching margin. Support can suggest sensible starting ranges once your account type is confirmed on Telegram.

One. Each membership licenses a single live MT5 account. During onboarding you register that specific account with us — the license is bound to it and cannot be used on a second account without contacting support.

MetaTrader 5 must stay running with the system attached for automated execution. Most users run MT5 on a VPS so execution is not interrupted when their laptop sleeps or travels. We can point you to common VPS setups during onboarding.

14

News is scanned every few hours; each item is weighted with time decay so recent headlines carry more influence. The live endpoint on this site reflects the current blended bias, score, and strength reading — visible in the Signal section without logging in.
