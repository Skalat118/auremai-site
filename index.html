The Signal
The Signal

One number that reads the whole room.

A monitoring process scans for high-relevance, gold-moving news every few hours — Fed and FOMC policy, CPI/PCE/NFP and other US macro data, major geopolitical shifts, and sudden unexplained moves in gold. A strict filter throws out speculation and stale news.

— Reading the signal…
Direction — 0 = bearish · 50 = neutral · 100 = bullish
Strength — how strong the signal is, regardless of direction

Updates automatically — no manual input.

  • Weighted by what actually moves gold

    Each item is tagged by category, in order of weight: Fed policy → key macro → major geopolitics → secondary macro → general — plus a bullish, bearish, or neutral bias.

  • Time-decay, not hard cutoffs

    A news item's influence halves roughly every six hours, so the signal glides smoothly instead of jumping when an item ages out.

  • From signal to position

    The trading system reads the signal per pending order and adjusts the risk tier — low, medium, or high — and the take-profit distance. Trades that run against the current signal use a separate, lower fixed tier.

FAQ

Questions, answered straight.

What is AuremAI?

AuremAI is an algorithmic trading system for gold (XAUUSD). It reads a live signal built from macro and geopolitical news and uses it to size every position dynamically. What you're buying is access to run the system, delivered and supported over Telegram.

How is the signal calculated?

A monitoring process scans for high-relevance gold news every few hours and tags each item with a category (Fed policy carries the most weight, down through key macro, major geopolitics, secondary macro, and general) and a bullish, bearish, or neutral bias. A live endpoint blends recent items using time-decay weighting — influence halves roughly every six hours — into a single bias label, a directional score, and a strength reading.

Can I see results before I buy?

Yes. The Track Record section above shows a live trading account — equity, balance, and history — that updates on its own. No purchase or sign-up required to watch it.

What risk is involved?

Trading carries real risk, including the loss of capital. AuremAI sizes positions to manage risk — using low, medium, and high risk tiers depending on signal strength — but managing risk is not the same as removing it. No system can guarantee a profit, and you should never trade money you can't afford to lose.

How do I receive the system after paying?

Everything happens over Telegram. After your access is confirmed, we walk you through installing and configuring the system on your own MetaTrader 5 terminal.

What's your refund policy?

Refunds are handled case by case. Reach out on Telegram and we'll talk it through with you directly.
